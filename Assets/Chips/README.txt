CHIP AND PLAQUE IMAGES
======================

Place your chip/plaque PNG images in this folder.

Expected files:
- chip_5c.png     (5 cent chip)
- chip_25c.png    (25 cent chip)
- chip_1.png      ($1 chip)
- chip_5.png      ($5 chip)
- plaque_25.png   ($25 plaque)
- plaque_100.png  ($100 plaque)

Image recommendations:
- Square images work best (e.g., 200x200 or 400x400 pixels)
- PNG format with transparent background
- Chips are typically round, plaques are typically rectangular

If images are missing, placeholder images will be generated automatically.
